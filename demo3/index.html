About the School

Clemson University is a public land-grant research university in Clemson, South Carolina. Founded in 1889, Clemson is the second-largest university by enrollment in South Carolina.

My Experience with Clemson

This is my fourth and final year at Clemson University. I loved this campus as soon as I stepped foot onto it. Everything about this school is great: the campus, the students, the sports, etc. The list just goes on and on. I'll be sad to leave this Spring but I'm glad to have spent the last 4 years here and I'm thankful for the education I received.
