Baseball

About the Sport

Baseball is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of nine players each, taking turns batting and fielding. The game occurs over the course of several plays, with each play generally beginning when a player on the fielding team, called the pitcher, throws a ball that a player on the batting team, called the batter, tries to hit with a bat. A run is scored when a runner crosses home plate.

I played baseball for most of my life from when I was in t-ball all the way up into high school. I played rec ball, travel ball, and school ball. It was always one of my favorite sports to play and I always love watching it as well.
